Sample preview — ADA Website Compliance Kit

Here’s an example of the structure your ADA kit will follow. Your final Word document will be generated with your business details and can be fully edited.

ABC Café — ADA Website Accessibility Statement (Excerpt)

  1. Accessibility Commitment – Clear statement that the website is intended to be accessible to all users, including people with disabilities.
  2. Standards Followed – Reference to WCAG 2.1 Level AA guidelines, semantic HTML, and assistive technology support.
  3. Ongoing Improvements – Regular audits, image alt-text checks, keyboard-only navigation testing, and remediation plans.
  4. How to Request Help – Email and phone details for users who need assistance or alternative formats.
  5. Feedback & Review – How feedback is handled and how often the statement is reviewed and updated.

Your generated Word kit will expand each section with editable paragraphs, bullet lists, and placeholders for your logo and contact information.

Frequently asked questions

What exactly do I receive after purchase?

For each kit, you receive a downloadable Microsoft Word document (.docx) containing structured sections, suggested clause language, and placeholders for your details. You can edit, re-brand, and adapt this file for your own organisation.

Do I have to fill in my business details?

Yes. After payment you’ll be asked for basic information such as your business name, industry, location, approximate number of workers, and any special risks or concerns. This helps us generate content that is more tailored to your context.

Is this legal advice or a guarantee of compliance?

No. Comply-Desk provides structured documents and policy language as an informational starting point. We do not provide legal advice and cannot guarantee that your business is fully compliant. Always review the documents with your own legal counsel or compliance team before implementation.

Can I reuse or regenerate the kit later?

You can reuse and edit your Word document as often as you like. If your operations change significantly, you can purchase a new kit or contact us to discuss options.

Who do I contact if something doesn’t work?

If you run into any issues with generation or downloads, please email enquiries@comply-desk.com with your order details and a brief description of the problem. We’ll do our best to help.
