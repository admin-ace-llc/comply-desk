Frequently asked questions

Is this legal advice?

No. Comply-Desk provides structured documents and policy language as a starting point. It is not legal advice or a guarantee of compliance. Always review with qualified counsel or your internal compliance team before implementation.

Which businesses is this for?

Comply-Desk is designed for U.S. small businesses that need clear, organized documentation but don’t have full-time compliance staff — e.g., cafés, studios, agencies, clinics, warehouses, and online businesses.

What file format do I receive?

You receive an outline on the page and a downloadable Word document (.docx) that you can customize, brand, and save in your own systems.

Can I reuse the kit if my business changes?

Yes. You can regenerate content by updating your answers, or edit the Word file directly if your operations or locations change.
