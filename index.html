Important notes & FAQ

Is this legal advice or guaranteed compliance?

No. Comply-Desk provides AI-generated templates and checklists aligned with widely accepted best practices. They are intended as customizable starting points only and are not legal advice or a guarantee of compliance.

Who is Comply-Desk for?

U.S.-based small businesses that need to level up their documentation fast—retail, restaurants, light manufacturing, offices, agencies, and online service businesses.

How do I get my kit?

After you pay and complete a short questionnaire, Comply-Desk uses AI to generate a structured plan and a downloadable Word (.docx) file. You can then adapt the document for your workplace.

Can I edit the content?

Yes. You should always review and adapt the content to your specific workplace, state requirements, industry practices, and legal counsel where available.
